Yunyoung Choi is a freshmen at the University of California Riverside. She is an international student from South Korea and is majoring in neuroscience. She has previous experience working in a fast food chain and has volunteered extensively in a soup kitchen helping out those in need. She has also volunteered translating Korean children's books into English helping to fill a library in Cambodia.

In 2021, Yunyoung Choi picked up the guitar and started songwriting. She quickly became interested in producing her own songs and in January 2023, released her first song, "Will to bleed" under the pseudonym Val Valet. She went on to release four more singles the same year including Get me away, Nevermind, I wouldn't be free, and last but not least, Pleasure. In 2024, she released her first full album, Idiot Kid. Inspired by a lyric of Elliot Smith, Idiot kid shares Yunyoung Choi's experience with friends, family, depression, and more. Wild Animals turned out to be a fan favorite as it has acquired the most streams on spotify. With deep influences form Alex G, Wild animals is a upbeat song that makes you nostalgic, happy, and sad all at the same time.
